## ❓ Q&A Prep (Top 5 Questions)

**Q1: "How do you handle fraud?"**
A: "Multi-layer verification, escrow system, dispute resolution, and user reviews. We're also implementing email verification and 2FA."

**Q2: "What's your competitive advantage?"**
A: "Real-time WebSocket technology that updates bids instantly. No competitor has this on mobile. Plus our modern UI and lower fees."

**Q3: "How will you acquire users?"**
A: "Referral program with 20% bonus, influencer partnerships, SEO, and partnerships with local auction houses. CAC target is $10."

**Q4: "Why Flutter?"**
A: "One codebase for iOS, Android, and Web. Saves 60% development time and cost. Native performance with beautiful UI."

**Q5: "What if eBay copies you?"**
A: "They're slow to innovate. We'll have first-mover advantage, better UX, and loyal user base. Plus we're targeting niches they ignore."
